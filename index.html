The Year of 2021

The 2020/21 School year has been a year like no other. Like many schools and students throughout the world, our 6th class girls hadn't set foot in our school since March of 2020. When they finally arrived back to fill our school once more with cheer and hope, they arrived back to an all together different school environment. Their classrooms had been completely changed, stickers and covid signs adorned the walls in every corridor, class bubbles and pods became the norm and even their teachers looked a little different as they wore masks to continue to fight the spread of Covid-19. Our 6th class girls knew they had their work cut out for them this year, both academically and socially as the role models for the younger classes in our school. To say they rose to the challenge would be an understatement. They have been phenomenal, adaptive, flexible and inspiring for all of our students and teachers. Many traditional highlgihts of their final year had to be cancelled or moved online. The usual clubs and team competitions abandoned. However, they remarkebly took this in their stride and percerviered through one of the most difficult school years in Mount Anville history. We couldn't be prouder of our students. We have full confindence that our graduates of the 2020/21 school year will continue to represent our school in such exemplary fashion and are destined for great things in the future.

Our School Story

Our school was founded by the society of the Sacred Heart and its origin dates from 1865. Mount Anville Junior School is a thriving catholic school with over 450 students.Sacred Heart education seeks to develop the whole person as a responsible member of society, confident of personal worth, actively living out Christ’s call to know, and share His Love. MAMJS, a Sacred Heart school aims to provide the environment for development in every sphere, spiritual, physical, emotional, social, intellectual, artistic and moral.
